Freshest Pizza in Utah Since 1996

Nate's pizza has been serving the Utah community delicious pizza since 1996. We are located in Draper at 1234 E Draper Parkway. The reason our pizza is so loved is because we always use the freshest ingredients and cook our pizza to perfection in a woodfire pizza oven. We have parterned with local produce and meat suppliers to always have fresh food. We provide a variety of toppings, prepared daily. Owned and operated by Nathan Nieman. If you would like to inquire for a employment please call us or come in and ask for an application.


Covid-19 Protocols















What we encourage our guests to do:

  • Wash your hands frequently and use paper towel to dry.

  • Avoid touching eyes, nose, and mouth.

  • Avoid close contact with people who are sick.

  • Stay at home when you are sick.

  • Cover your nose and mouth when you cough or sneeze.

What we are doing:

  • Employees must always wear a mask.

  • Hand sanitizer and temperature check upon each employee clocking in.

  • Avoiding close contact with all employees.

  • Staying at home when an employee or employees' family member are sick.

  • Sanitizing and cleaning restaraunt every hour.
